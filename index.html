Bio

I am a third-year Ph.D student at Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong, supervised by Dr. Linqi Song . Prior to that, I obtained my bachelor degree from Xi’an Jiaotong University in 2018. I previously interned at Tencent AI Lab, advised by Dr. Kun Xu. My research interests are lied on dialogue system and semantic parsing.
